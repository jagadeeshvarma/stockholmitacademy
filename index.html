Payment and course questions

Here are answers to common questions before you join. These help you know what you receive, how the course works and what to expect after you pay.

What happens after I pay

You get a Stripe receipt immediately. Within one working day our team sends a welcome mail with your batch schedule, meeting links, study plan and next steps. You do not need to do anything else.

Do I need any previous experience

Manual Testing and Spoken English do not need any background. Python and JavaScript need basic comfort with a computer. Automation Testing and the combined track need some idea of testing or coding, but we guide you step by step.

How are classes conducted

All sessions are live on Zoom with real tasks, examples and feedback. You also receive access to recordings and support through mail or community channels.

Will I get study material

Yes. Every course includes notes, assignments, real examples and guided practice tasks. These stay with you even after the course ends.

Do you help with job preparation

Yes. Every learner receives guidance for LinkedIn, resume, interview preparation and assignment help. If you join our testing or coding courses, you also get project level practice that supports real job applications.

How do I choose the right course

Manual Testing is good for starting a testing career. Automation Testing is good if you want coding plus testing. Python and JavaScript are good for programming careers. Spoken English is for interviews and workplace communication.

Our promise to every learner

When you join a Stockholm IT Academy course you receive clear structure, practical tasks and steady support. We keep your learning simple, real and focused.

Weekly plan so you always know what to learn and practice Real project tasks that reflect how teams work in companies Courses updated each batch so new learners get the latest examples and guidance Friendly support through mail and the community when you need help Awarded Educator of the Year in 2024 and nominated again in 2025 Learners from our testing and coding tracks now working in well known teams in Sweden
